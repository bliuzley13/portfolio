Sunshine Kids Afterschool and Daycare

I worked here as a teacher's assistant. In the period of time that I volunteered there, I gained many social skills from individuals of various age groups. Furthermore, I learned how to interact with people more comfortably and grew as a person. I began to understand office politics and get a glimpse of what the real world was like. Asides from the valuable experience, I made many friends and met so many new faces. The atmosphere was really nice, and I liked to work there because of it. The place was comfortable for the students as well as the teachers who were there to teach the kids. My position was full of non-stop work; however, this experience was essential to the fact that I got training for things in various aspects that saves me time. Seeing new things began to show me newer perspectives, because of the point of view from various individuals, no matter the age.
